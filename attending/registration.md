### Registration Information for authors of accepted papers (Research/Doctoral Track)

- Authors must register by the early bird deadline for their paper to be included in the proceedings.
- An accepted paper in the main track will be published in the proceedings only if the final camera-ready version is accompanied by the payment of at least one AUTHOR Regular REGISTRATION type (either IEEE Member or Non - IEEE Member) for one of the authors (even if all authors of the paper are students, i.e., a student registration does not cover any paper).
- Only accepted paper in the doctoral track can be covered by a student author registration.
- Each author registration covers a maximum of one paper.
- IEEE reserves the right to exclude a paper from distribution after the conference, including IEEE Xplore Digital Library, if the paper is not presented by the author at the conference.

### Early Birds Registration (Until April 17th)

All Registration fees are in Canadian dollars (CAD) and include  VAT (13%). All types of registration cover admission to all sessions, coffee breaks, lunch, and social events

- Author Registration (including publication fees)
  - Main track:
    - IEEE member: $1,250
    - Non-IEEE member: $1,400
  - Doctoral track:
    - Student registration (IEEE): $950
    - Student registration (non-IEEE): $1,050
- Regular Participants
  - IEEE member: $950
  - Non-IEEE member: $1,050
  - Student (IEEE): $700
  - Student (non-IEEE): $750
  - IEEE Lifetime member: $700
