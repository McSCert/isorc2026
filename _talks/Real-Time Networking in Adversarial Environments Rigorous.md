Abstract
--------
The majority of embedded systems used in industry require either hard or soft real-time constraints. Meeting these requirements with the current design workflow requires a significant effort in design and verification. In this paper, we propose a tailored methodology for designing, implementing, and analyzing hard real-time systems. Our method is the integration of time-predictable T-CREST hardware platform and Lingua Franca deterministic language and runtime framework. We evaluate our proposed method using the Platin worst-case execution time (WCET) analyzer and demonstrate its correctness.
