        <h2>About ISORC 2026</h2>
        <p>
            The IEEE International Symposium on Real-Time Computing (ISORC) is a premier forum for 
            researchers and practitioners to exchange ideas and present results on real-time computing 
            systems, real-time applications, and enabling technologies.
        </p>
        <p>
            ISORC 2026 will bring together experts from academia and industry to discuss the latest 
            advances in real-time computing, including real-time operating systems, scheduling algorithms, 
            quality of service, middleware, distributed systems, and embedded systems.
        </p>
