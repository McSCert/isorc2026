Call for Papers

Submit Your Research to ISORC 2026

About the Conference

The IEEE International Symposium on Real-Time Computing (ISORC) invites submissions of original research papers on all aspects of real-time computing systems. We welcome theoretical and experimental papers that address the design, implementation, and evaluation of real-time systems and applications.

Topics of Interest

  • Real-time scheduling and schedulability analysis
  • Real-time operating systems
  • Embedded and cyber-physical systems
  • Time-sensitive networking
  • Mixed-criticality systems
  • Multicore and multiprocessor real-time systems
  • Real-time middleware and distributed systems
  • Quality of Service (QoS) management
  • Resource management and allocation
  • Energy-aware real-time systems
  • Real-time virtualization
  • Timing analysis and verification
  • Internet of Things (IoT) and edge computing
  • Autonomous systems and robotics
  • Industrial applications and case studies
  • Safety-critical and fault-tolerant systems
  • Real-time artificial intelligence and machine learning
  • Automotive and avionics systems

Submission Guidelines

Paper Format

  • Papers must be written in English
  • Use IEEE conference format (double-column)
  • Maximum length: 10 pages (including references)
  • Papers must be original and not previously published
  • All submissions will undergo double-blind peer review

Submission Process

  • Submit papers through EasyChair (link TBA)
  • Remove all author information for blind review
  • Include title, abstract, and keywords
  • Accepted papers will be published in IEEE Xplore
