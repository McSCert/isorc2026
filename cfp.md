<section class="cfp-intro">

        <h2>About the Conference</h2>
        <p>
            The IEEE International Symposium on Real-Time Computing (ISORC) invites submissions of 
            original research papers on all aspects of real-time computing systems. We welcome 
            theoretical and experimental papers that address the design, implementation, and 
            evaluation of real-time systems and applications.
        </p>
    </div>
</section>

<section class="submission-guidelines">
    <div class="container">
        <h2>Submission Guidelines</h2>
        <div class="guidelines-box">
            <h3>Paper Format</h3>
            <ul>
                <li>Papers must be written in English</li>
                <li>Use IEEE conference format (double-column)</li>
                <li>Maximum length: 10 pages (including references)</li>
                <li>Papers must be original and not previously published</li>
                <li>All submissions will undergo double-blind peer review</li>
            </ul>
            
            <h3 style="margin-top: 2rem;">Submission Process</h3>
            <ul>
                <li>Submit papers through EasyChair (link TBA)</li>
                <li>Remove all author information for blind review</li>
                <li>Include title, abstract, and keywords</li>
                <li>Accepted papers will be published in IEEE Xplore</li>
            </ul>
        </div>
    </div>
</section>
